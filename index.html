    Introduction


    The Foundation consists of seven directors who are joined together by the common vision of operating a charitable organisation centred around the medical condition of cerebral palsy, which our late daughter and sister, Maselaelo Maria Motuku, had suffered from.

    Vision


    Our vision is to establish and operate a sustainable foundation that caters primarily for the needs of people who are affected by cerebral palsy, but also those who are faced with a variety of other life challenges.

    Mission


    Through the Foundation, and relying on personal experiences and the reasearch that we have gathered, we hope to be able to effectively and efficiently bring a positive difference to other people's lives.

Values


  • People are our number one priority. This core value is succinctly instilled in the Northern Sotho idiom, “Feta kgomo, o tshware motho”.

  • Health is our greatest wealth. Our long-term vision is to ensure a healthy lifestyle for those we aim to affect through the Foundation.

  • Absolute integrity.

Our Business


  • The Foundation will require strategic partners in order for it become a success.

  • Potential stakeholders for the Foundation will include other non-profit organisations, public and private companies, and individuals to sponsor the various projects and day-to-day operations of the Foundation.

  • Potential stakeholders will also include those whose needs form part of the Foundation's vision and mission.
